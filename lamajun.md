# Lahm'b'Ajeen

A delightful savory meat pizza-like thing.  Think Man'ousheh but with meat and for dinner.

Follow the instructions for making [Man'ousheh dough here](manaeesh.md) however instead of the Zaatar mixture you'll be making a meat mixture to top the dough before baking it.

## Ingredients
- [ ] 1 lb ground beef or lamb
- [ ] 2 cups finely chopped roma tomatoes (romas have less liquid)
- [ ] 2 cups finely chopped onions
- [ ] 4 garlic cloves
- [ ] 1/4 cup pine nuts
- [ ] 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
- [ ] 1 tablespoon tomato paste
- [ ] 2 tablespoons pomegranate molasses (optional - I usually skip it, but using it will give you the more traditional tart flavor).
- [ ] 2 tablespoons [pepper paste](https://www.amazon.com/s?k=hot+pepper+paste+turkish) this is a Turkish product - you should adjust to suit your spice level.
- [ ] 1 tablespoon [tahini](https://www.amazon.com/EAST-SESAME-SPREAD-TAHINI-PLASTIC/dp/B07FM2N6T8?source=ps-sl-shoppingads-lpcontext&ref_=fplfs&psc=1&smid=AA9C1GM513EBY)
- [ ] 2 tablespoons greek yoghurt or [labneh](https://www.karouncheese.com/karoun-labne) (I recommend the labneh if you can find it)
- [ ] 2 teaspoons salt
- [ ] 2 teaspoons [seven-spice blend]((https://www.amazon.com/Ziyad-All-Natural-Flavorful-Additives-Preservatives/dp/B005SFGUOW)) (see [here](kafta-in-tahini.md#seven-spice-blend) for details/substitutions)

## Method

1. With [mortar and pestle](https://www.amazon.com/s?k=wooden+mortar+and+pestle&crid=1BUF1A6X31XDC&sprefix=wooden+mortar+and+pestl%2Caps%2C126&ref=nb_sb_noss_2) mash together garlic, pine nuts, black pepper and 1/2 the salt.
2. Fry up onions in olive oil till wilted.
3. Add ground beef and cook until done (no pink remains).  Drain most (not necessarily all) of excess fat.
4. Add roma tomatoes and cook down.
5. Add mashed garlic and pine nut mixture and cook, stirring constantly, until aromatic (about 30 seconds).
6. Add all remaining ingredients (tomato paste, pomegranate molasses, pepper paste, tahini, labneh, remaining 1tsp salt, 2 tsp seven-spice blend) and combine.  Let simmer for 2-3 minutes so it all comes together.
7. Take of heat and let cool (e.g. while dough is rising).

Proceed to make flatbreads following the mana'eesh recipe substituting ~2 Tbsp meat mixture per for zaatar.  Fold in half as they cool.  Serve with [labneh](https://www.karouncheese.com/karoun-labne), olives, tomatoes, mint, and pickles.
