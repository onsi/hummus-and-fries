# Sitto's Pasta Sauce

My grandmother, Sitto Cosette, came up with this simple but delicious - and distinctly Lebanese - pasta sauce.

## Ingredients

- [ ] 1 large red onion, chopeped.
- [ ] 3 tablespoons olive oil
- [ ] 4 cloves garlic, minced
- [ ] 1 lb 93% ground beef
- [ ] 1/3 cup pine nuts
- [ ] 1 can tomato sauce
- [ ] 1 teaspoon black pepper
- [ ] 1 teaspoon cinnamon
- [ ] 1/2 teaspoon salt

## Method

1. Cook up the onions in oil.
1. When the onion start wilting, add the beef and cook until done breaking it up as you go.
1. Add garlic and pine nuts.  Stir well as you fry until just aromatic (30 seconds).
1. Add tomato sauce and water to cover.
1. Simmer for 10 minutes.
1. Add spices and continue to simmer for 10+ minutes.
1. Add more water to get desired consistency.
1. Serve with pasta and parmesan cheese.
