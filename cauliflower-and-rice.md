# Cauliflower and Rice

Super simple, but delicious!, vegetarian fare.

## Ingredients

- [ ] Cauliflower (1 large-sized head, separated into chunks - between 1 and 2 inches in diameter)
- [ ] 1 red onion (diced)
- [ ] 4 cloves garlic (minced fine)
- [ ] 1/3 cup pine nuts
- [ ] Coriander powder (1 tsp)
- [ ] Turmeric (1/2 tsp)
- [ ] Salt (1/4 tsp + scant 1/2 tsp)
- [ ] Ground black pepper (1/4 tsp + 1/4 tsp)
- [ ] Lemon juice (1.5 - 2 lemons)
- [ ] Olive oil

## Method

1. Place cut cauliflower in bowl and toss in olive oil with 1/4 salt and 1/4 tsp pepper.
2. Place on parchment-sheet lined baking pan and bake at 400ºF until golden and caramelized - roughly 20-25 minutes, turning halfway through baking.
3. In pot fry diced onions and 1/4 tsp pepper in oil until soft and yellow in color.
4. Add minced garlic, coriander powder, trumeric, pine nuts, salt.  Fry until aromatic (30s).
5. Add the roasted cauliflower and enough water to cover.
6. Bring to boil then simmer.  Cook, uncovered, until tender (30-40 minutes).
7. Stir in lemon juice before serving.  Taste and adjust lemon and salt.

Serve with rice.  This goes really well with a simple cabbage, cucumber, and tomato salad (for a simple dressing: olive oil, crushed garlic, salt, pepper, and lemon).
